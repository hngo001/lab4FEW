Literary Terms Glossary

The American Dream
The cultural concept of achieving success and prosperity through hard work and determination.
Symbolism
The use of symbols to represent ideas or qualities within the novel, such as the green light or the Valley of Ashes.
Tragedy
A genre where the main character experiences downfall due to personal flaws or external factors.
